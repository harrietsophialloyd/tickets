FAQ

    How do I Create a Ticket?



    You will need to lauch the application select your department and the category your issues comes under and click the go button.

    How long is the response time?



    We will try to respond and provide an update as soon as possible. You will also be able to see whos got your ticket.

    Can I track my tickets created?



    You will be able to track your submitted questions under the My Questions Tab. Also you will be able to view all of the questions asked by other employees to help you resolve your issue.

    Can my ticket be esculated if the team can not resolve it?



    At the moment you will not be able to esculate your ticket but this is a feature which will be coming soon.

    Can I create a urgent ticket?



    At the moment you will not be able to set priority to a ticket but this will be a feature which will be coming soon.
